Overview

Designed and built a complete Webflow platform for Yar Ventures — a VC fund that needed to look as credible as the companies it backs, and scale as fast as its portfolio grows.

38+ portfolio companies filterable in real time across stage, vertical, and geography
4 CMS collections interconnected — one entry updates the filter page, logo marquee, and event archive automatically
Non-technical team can manage the entire site independently, with no code changes ever needed

Challenge

A VC fund with no digital presence

Yar Ventures had a growing portfolio, a community of Silicon Valley LPs, and monthly pitch events — but no website that could communicate its scale or credibility. Investors needed a way to browse companies; the team needed a system they could update themselves.

Approach

System design first, visuals second

Built around a multi-collection CMS schema before a single visual was designed. The portfolio filter system — stage, vertical, HQ, and live search — was architected using Webflow CMS multi-reference fields and Finsweet CMS Filter to deliver a product-grade experience.

Outcome

A site that runs itself

The fund's team now adds a new portfolio company in minutes. The homepage marquee, filter page, and all listing views update automatically. The design signals institutional trust — and the system behind it is built to scale to hundreds of companies.

Design

Credibility without coldness

The visual direction had to communicate trust and forward momentum — qualities investors expect from a fund, but rarely feel from a startup-built site. The palette is minimal: off-white, dark ink, and a single red accent used only for emphasis and active states. Typography is set in Cormorant Garamond for all headings — a choice that reads editorial rather than corporate.

Every section was designed to disappear: the experience should feel like the content, not the design.

Homepage — bold stat counters build instant credibility; portfolio logo marquee signals scale

CMS Architecture

One update. Everywhere.

Designed for scalability — a single CMS entry propagates across the filter page, logo marquee, event archive, and any future views automatically. The team adds a new portfolio company in minutes. The site handles the rest.

Portfolio Companies

Name, description, stage, vertical, HQ, logo, external link — feeds the filter page and homepage marquee

Team Members

Photo, name, role, bio — structured for future team growth without layout changes

Synergy Events

Flyer image, date, registration link — powers the homepage event section and archive

Portfolio Logos

Drives the animated homepage marquee — add a company, logo appears automatically
