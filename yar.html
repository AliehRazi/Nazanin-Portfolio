Portfolio Summary

A full Webflow build for a Silicon Valley VC fund — balancing institutional credibility with community warmth. The centrepiece is a real-time, three-axis portfolio filter system across 38+ companies, backed by a scalable multi-collection CMS that runs the entire site without touching code.

Design

The visual direction needed to feel trusted and forward-looking — a fund that is rigorous but community-driven. A restrained palette, clean typographic hierarchy, and structured white space signal credibility without feeling cold or corporate.

Homepage — hero with impact stats and portfolio logo marquee

CMS Architecture

Four interconnected CMS collections power the entire site — one new entry populates the filter page, logo marquee, and any future embeds automatically.

Portfolio Companies — name, description, stage, vertical, HQ, logo, link
Team Members — photo, role, bio (About page)
Synergy Events — flyer, date, registration link
Portfolio Logos — feeds the homepage marquee
