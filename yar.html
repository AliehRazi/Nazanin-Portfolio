Yar

A community-driven AI & DeepTech investment platform for Silicon Valley professionals.

Webflow UI Design Branding

About

Yar is a curated investment platform built for Persian-speaking professionals in Silicon Valley — a space where community meets capital. The challenge was to make something that felt both trusted and forward-looking.

The design leans into a refined, editorial aesthetic — generous white space, structured typography, and a restrained color palette that signals credibility without feeling corporate.
